Welcome!

This is the website of the ACL Special Interest Group on Typology (SIGTYP)

The purpose the SIG is to promote interest in Typology in NLP/CL, to provide members of the ACL with a special interest in Typology with a means of exchanging news of recent research developments and other matters of interest in typology, and to organize annual workshops collocated with *ACL/ALT conferences.

Recent and Upcoming Events

Typ-NLP2019

The first workshop on Typology for Polyglot Natural Language Processing was co-located with ACL2019 in Florence, Italy.

Typ-NLP2020

The second workshop on Typology for Polyglot Natural Language Processing will be co-located with EMNLP2020 in Punta Cana, Dominican Republic.
